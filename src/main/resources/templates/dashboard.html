Welcome to HDFC Bank

Bank customer information includes a customer's personal and account information, and is used for a variety of purposes, including:

Identification: Banks use customer information to identify customers and comply with international regulations against money laundering and terrorist financing. This is done through a Know Your Customer (KYC) policy, which includes customer identification procedures, risk assessment, and ongoing monitoring.

Marketing: Banks use customer information to create targeted marketing campaigns

.

Customer service: Banks use customer information to respond to customer inquiries and provide personalized service
